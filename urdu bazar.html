The History of Lahore

Lahore is the capital of Pakistan's. Punjab province, boasts a 2,000 to 4,000-year history, originating from ancient Hindu legends as Lavapuri. It rose to prominence under Muslim rule in the 10th century, became a "Golden Age" Mughal capital, was the center of the 19th-century Sikh Empire, and is now Pakistan’s cultural heart.
